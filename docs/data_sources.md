# Data Sources

## Commodity Prices
- **Source**: World Bank Pink Sheet
- **URL**: https://www.worldbank.org/en/research/commodity-markets
- **Coverage**: 2000-2025, Monthly
- **Variables**: Crude oil (Brent, Dubai), Natural gas, Wheat, Rice, Copper, Aluminum

## Bilateral Trade Data
- **Source**: UN Comtrade
- **URL**: https://uncomtrade.org/
- **Coverage**: 2010-2024, Monthly
- **Partners**: USA, China, EU27, Saudi Arabia, UAE, Qatar
- **Commodities**: HS 27 (Energy), 10-11 (Food), 74 (Copper), 76 (Aluminum)

## Input-Output Tables
- **Source**: MOSPI (Ministry of Statistics India)
- **URL**: https://mospi.gov.in/
- **Version**: 2015-16, 2020-21
- **Sectors**: 139 detailed sectors

## Macroeconomic Data
- **Source**: Reserve Bank of India
- **URL**: https://dbie.rbi.org.in/
- **Variables**: IIP, WPI, GDP, Exchange Rate

## Global Indicators
- **Source**: OECD Data Explorer
- **URL**: https://data-explorer.oecd.org/
- **Variables**: GDP growth, commodity indices, trade volumes
